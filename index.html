RECIPIES & DISHES


Biryani

Biryani is a delicious and flavorful Indian dish made with fragrant rice, aromatic spices, and tender meat or vegetables. Here's a basic recipe for Chicken Biryani:

Click to see the Ingredients
    For Marinating Chicken:
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
    For Rice:
  • 2 cups long-grain Basmati rice
  • 4-5 cups water
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste
    For Biryani:

  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron strands (soaked in 2 tablespoons warm milk)
  • 2 tablespoons fried onions (for garnish)
  • Ghee (clarified butter) for drizzling (optional)

Marag

Marag is a Middle Eastern dish made with marinated and roasted meat, typically served in pita bread or flatbreads with a variety of toppings and sauces. Here's a recipe for Chicken.:

Click to see the Ingredients
    For Marinating Chicken:

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs or breast, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
    For the Garlic Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tahini (sesame paste)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons plain yogurt
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for thinning, as needed)
    For Serving:

  • Pita bread or flatbreads
  • Sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped

Mandi

Mutton Mandi is a flavorful Arabian rice dish that's known for its aromatic spices and tender mutton. Here's a recipe to make Mutton Mandi::

Click to see the Ingredients
    For the Mutton Marinade:

  • 500 grams of bone-in mutton pieces
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons mandi spice mix (readily available or make your own blend)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • Salt to taste
    For the Mandi Rice:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup raisins or dried apricots (optional)
  • Salt to taste
    For the Mandi Spice Mix (You can also buy pre-made Mandi spice mix):

  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon ground cardamom
  • 1 tablespoon ground cloves
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon ground bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg
